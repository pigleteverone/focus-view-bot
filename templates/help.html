Help & Game Guide

Welcome to the Gambling Game Bot! Here's how to get started:

  1. Create an account - Register to receive 1,000 starting coins and begin your gambling journey.
  2. Check your profile - View your current stats, balance, and game history.
  3. Collect daily rewards - Don't forget to claim your daily bonus by clicking the "Claim Daily" button on your profile.
  4. Work for more coins - Every 10 minutes, you can work to earn additional coins.
  5. Play games - Choose from our selection of gambling games to win big!
  6. Level up - Winning games gives you XP, which increases your level and daily rewards.
The higher your level, the more coins you'll receive from daily rewards and work!

Coin Flip
  • Choose either heads or tails
  • Place your bet
  • If your prediction is correct, you win 2x your bet
  • If wrong, you lose your bet
  • 50/50 odds of winning
Blackjack
  • The goal is to get cards totaling closer to 21 than the dealer without going over
  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10
  • Aces are worth 11 or 1, whichever is better for your hand
  • If you win, you get 2x your bet
  • If you tie (push), your bet is returned
  • If you lose, you lose your bet
Slots
  • Place your bet and spin the slots
  • Three matching symbols wins big - payouts range from 5x to 20x your bet
  • Two matching symbols win 1x your bet
  • Different symbols have different payout values
  • No matches means you lose your bet

Earning Coins
  • Starting Balance: 1,000 coins for new players
  • Daily Reward: 1,000 + (level × 100) coins every 24 hours
  • Work: 500 + (level × 50) coins every 10 minutes
  • Winning Games: Different payouts depending on the game and bet amount
Experience (XP) System
  • Win games to earn XP (typically 100 XP per win)
  • XP needed for next level = (current level + 1) × 1,000
  • Higher levels increase your daily and work rewards
  • There's no level cap, so you can keep progressing indefinitely
Pro Tip: Balance risk and reward! While high bets can win big, they can also lead to big losses. Consider your betting strategy carefully.

Q: What happens if I run out of coins?

A: You can still claim your daily reward and work every 10 minutes to rebuild your balance. Don't worry, you'll be back in the game soon!

Q: How is the house edge calculated?

A: Each game has different odds:

  • Coin Flip: 50/50 odds with no house edge
  • Blackjack: Slight house edge due to dealer rules
  • Slots: Higher house edge balanced by larger potential payouts
Q: Can I transfer coins to another player?

A: Currently, player-to-player transfers are not supported.

Q: How do I level up faster?

A: Win games consistently! Each win gives you XP, regardless of bet size. Playing frequently is the key to leveling up quickly.

Q: Are more games coming soon?

A: Yes! We're constantly working on adding new games and features to enhance your gambling experience.
